About Us

Speedpack Express, Inc. was founded in 2000 by logistics professionals with over 25 years in the industry. We started by providing ground transportation in Miami and Ft. Lauderdale for courier companies and freight forwarders, growing steadily through trial and error—arguably the best teachers. Over a decade later, largely through word of mouth, we became the pickup and delivery agent of choice in South Florida. Our principles remain the same: keep service levels high and costs fair.

Our Company

Available 24 hours a day, 365 days a year. We handle time-sensitive pickups and deliveries, from organs for transplants to critical parts, documents, and more. We offer hands-on monitoring, GPS tracking, and on-time delivery for impossible deadlines—the courier’s courier, serving companies and forwarders for over a decade.

Our Vision

To deliver the highest-quality transportation and logistics services for time-critical shipments in South Florida. We develop our staff and infrastructure so customer requests are never refused. In complex situations we combine and adapt resources to meet expectations. Communication—internal and with customers—is our backbone. We work as one team, confident in our abilities and supported by the entire Speedpack organization.
