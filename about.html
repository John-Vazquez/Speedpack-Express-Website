WHO WE ARE

The courier’s courier.

Founded in 2000, Speedpack Express has grown through hands-on logistics work, reliable communication, and consistent service for courier companies, freight forwarders, and time-sensitive customers across Miami, Fort Lauderdale, and the surrounding South Florida market.

OUR MISSION

Move fast. Communicate clearly. Deliver reliably.

We focus on shipments where timing matters. From airport cargo recoveries to urgent deliveries and transfers, our team is built to respond quickly, adapt when plans change, and keep customers informed from pickup to delivery.
